We commit to our values for decades now and decades yet to come.

Who We Are

Window Corner has more than 40 years of experience in façade and windows field. First, we started in 1976 as AluBakir, a family business that offers aluminum works and fabrications. We were among the first companies to execute curtain walls and use high-end brands of aluminum profiles. Our aim has always been to be the ultimate solution provider to reach an optimum combination in terms of functionality and appearance. In 1998, with the introduction of PVC as an alternative material into the Egyptian market, we established a shareholding company called “Online” and extended our product range to include Aluminum and PVC. Online was also the first company in Egypt and the Middle East to use Fiberglass. Keeping our vision to offer the best solution for all sectors, Window Corner, a new brand, was introduced in 2012. It is specialized in residential facilities to serve individual clients with all glass works at home. Window Corner has a high level of customization and a dedicated team to meet each individual’s own requirements. We always seek to give an added value and offer our consultation to our clients to build a unique cozy home.

Our Values

Window Corner is a brand made exclusively for individuals to be able to receive a well-tailored solution depending on their own house. Our key values are Trust and Integrity. We work on building a long term relationship with our clients to play a partner and advisor role on all levels starting from design till after-sales maintenance. We never encounter any conflict while doing so, because Window Corner is a fabricator, capable of using all kinds of products to fulfil the client’s desire in terms of Aesthetics, Safety & Security, Performance and Cost Optimization. We have worked with more than 50 gated community compounds around Egypt including the most prestigious developers like Sodic, Palm Hills, New Giza, Emaar, Swan Lake, Kattameya Heights and Lake View.
